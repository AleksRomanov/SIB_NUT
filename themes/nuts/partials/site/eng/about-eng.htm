«Siberian company»

Our company OOO “Siberian nut” was founded in 2015. The base and the head office are located in Karsnoyarsk Krai that is very close to main pine nut harvest territories in Russia.

«With care from Siberia»

We specialize in the processing of the pine nut harvested in ecologically pure areas of rich Siberian Taiga. We take care about the quality of our product in order to let our clients to fill up with real Siberian health.

«Siberian facilities»

Our main focus is on shelled pine nut kernels production. We process more than 2000 mt of raw material and get more than 800 mt of kernels.
